Michael Grant is 67 years old and was born on 26th July 1954 in Los Angeles. He has lived in over 50 homes in 14 states. He moved in with his wife Katherine Applegate after knowing her for only a day! He and Katherine started their own cleaning buisness and in their spare time they started writing their own book. Now they have written over 150 books in total and are know for the Gone, BZRK and Front Lines. None of their books have been turned into films (Yet) but BZRK have got a movie deal and Gone may be turned into a tv series.
